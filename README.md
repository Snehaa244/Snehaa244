## 👩‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Software Engineer** with a deep passion for **Frontend Development** and **UI/UX Designing**. I have extensive hands-on experience in creating **modern, user-friendly, and beautiful web interfaces**. My love for **designing** made me skilled in tools like **Figma, Canva, Sketch, Blender, Spline, and Adobe Suite**.

I'm also highly skilled in **React.js, Next.js, GSAP, Three.js, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, Chart.js, React Rechart, React Router, C, C++, Power BI, and more**.

💎 **Achievements:**
- 🏆 **Won 2 National Level Hackathons.**
- 💻 **Cleared multiple rounds of competitive coding contests.**
- 🎨 **Worked on several design-based freelance projects.**

I'm highly enthusiastic about working with **foreign clients** and delivering **professional and creative solutions**.
